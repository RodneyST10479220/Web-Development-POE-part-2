Who We Are

EcoPedal Deliveries is a passionate, community-driven delivery service founded to provide fast, affordable, and environmentally sustainable deliveries across townships and urban areas. We use bicycles instead of motor vehicles to reduce emissions, improve traffic flow, and create jobs within local communities.


Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver goods in the most eco-friendly way possible, while supporting local economies and encouraging sustainable transport alternatives. We aim to make a positive environmental and social impact with every delivery we make.


Our Vision

We envision a cleaner, greener future where bicycles are at the heart of last-mile delivery systems in townships and cities across the country.


Our Values

  • Sustainability – Reducing our carbon footprint through pedal-powered transport.
  • Community Empowerment – Creating job opportunities and investing in township development.
  • Reliability – Ensuring every package arrives quickly and safely.
  • Innovation – Constantly improving our services and delivery methods.

Meet the Team

Our team includes experienced cyclists, logistics professionals, and passionate community members who believe in the power of green delivery. Every rider is trained to handle deliveries with care and commitment.
